Discover Viet Nam

A Hidden Gem in Southeast Asia

Vietnam is a beautiful country located in Southeast Asia, sitting on the eastern edge of the Indochina Peninsula. It shares its land borders with three unique neighbors: China to the far north, and Laos and Cambodia to the west. On its eastern and southern sides, the country opens up to the vast, blue waters of the East Sea. This great location makes Vietnam a central hub in the region, offering a wonderful mix of diverse landscapes, from tall northern mountains to flat, green river lands in the sunny south, perfect for any traveler to explore.

The Beautiful Shape and Coastline

When you look at Vietnam on a worlsd map, you will immediately notice its fascinating shape, which looks exactly like the letter "S". The country is very long and narrow, stretching over a total land area of about 331,000 square kilometers, which is similar in size to Germany. Because of this unique shape, Vietnam boasts an incredibly long and beautiful coastline that curves for more than 3,260 kilometers. This endless coast is home to hundreds of peaceful fishing villages, dramatic rocky cliffs, and world-famous sandy beaches that welcome millions of tourists every year.
